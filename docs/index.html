Imprint

Caption your TIFFs in a single drop.

Download Imprint Apple Silicon Mac · macOS 14 or later

How it works

1

Gather

Put your .tif photos and your caption file — Excel or CSV — in the same folder.

2

Drop

Drag the folder into Imprint, or click to choose it.

3

Done

Captions land in the Description field, ready for Bridge, Photoshop, Lightroom, agencies.
